Member's View

A personal digital space where every member sees herself at the heart of her family network. The AI Companion Arc stays close, quietly available; family nodes reflect connection and care. Tapping navigates to health data, voice, and relationship depth.

✦
Hana Chen · 68 years old
Retired homemaker · Taipei · Grandma in the Chen family
Senior member Tech-light Health-conscious Fraud-vulnerable Family-connected
Design Principles
1
Self at center — Hana sees herself at the heart of her family network, surrounded by her loved ones. The design reinforces her agency and equal standing within the constellation.
2
AI Companion Arc as ambient presence — The AI arc at the top conveys care without intruding. It's always present, quietly available — tapping reveals the AI's actions for the day.
3
Relational depth on tap — Each family node is a doorway: voice messages, shared memories, or live health updates. Navigating is exploratory, not task-driven.
4
Health as context, not alarm — The health report is accessible but presented calmly. Data is shown with context and AI recommendations, never as raw numbers without meaning.
5
Voice as primary input — For seniors, voice is more natural than tapping. The Your Voice screen allows hands-free navigation and messaging.

Mei Chen · 45 years old
Brand Manager · Taipei · Connected to Kenji, Yuki, Hiro & Grandma
Working mom Family anchor Health ally Risk-aware Delegates to AI
Design Principles
1
Mei at the center — Mei sees herself at the heart of her family network. Each node is a living connection — a relationship she stays close to, not just a contact list.
2
Proactive alerts — Three stacked alert pills appear at the top right, surfacing urgent issues before Mei looks for them. Each is independently dismissible once reviewed.
3
Per-member depth — Tapping any family node opens that person's wellbeing and security updates. No member's latest news is more than one tap away.
4
AI Companion Arc as action gateway — The arc navigates directly to Kaleidoscope chat — the highest-frequency action for Mei as a coordinator. The arc is both a reassurance symbol and a shortcut.
5
Events as shared family timeline — The middle tab shows a chronological family-wide activity log, giving everyone a single view of what happened across the network without opening each profile.
6
Read-state lifecycle — Alert nodes have three states: unread (pulsing ring, circle clickable), read-active within 12h (circle dimmed, status chips become the entry point), and resolved (fully dimmed, ✓ badge). All past events collect in the Kaleidoscope History tab.

Creator's View

A young creator's personal world — Yuki at center, family around her, and a gateway to Bloom built right into the constellation. TrendLife bridges Yuki's creative ambitions with the family's trust layer, making parental involvement feel natural rather than restrictive.

Yv
Yuki Chen · 16 years old
High school student · Taipei · Mei & Kenji's daughter, Hiro's sister
Teen creator Fashion & Art Digitally native Values autonomy AI-friendly
Design Principles
1
Creator at center — Yuki sees herself as the main character of her own world. The constellation reflects her creative identity and her connections — not a view managed by parents.
2
Bloom as a constellation node — Bloom appears as a discoverable node below Yuki, connected by a dashed purple line. Tapping triggers a zoom launch transition into Bloom's onboarding flow. The Bloom icon uses a purple double-ring mandala — a sister icon to Kaleidoscope.
3
Parental approval without friction — When an under-18 gate is hit, TrendLife routes the consent request directly to Mei via Kaleidoscope — no email, no separate app. Yuki sees a waiting screen with real-time delivery confirmation.
4
Transparency, not surveillance — The consent screen tells Yuki exactly what parents can see (activity summary, time limits, safety alerts) and what they can't (full content detail). Trust is built through clarity.
5
Kaleidoscope as Yuki's ally — The AI Companion Arc uses Yuki's color (green) and language. It's her helper, not a parental watchdog embedded in her view.
