# Proposal Briefs

---

## The Constellation
https://trendlife-constellation.vercel.app/constellation-spec.html

- Tap a node to expand that person's status and threads in place — no navigation, just depth
- Each member unlocks their own constellation to explore — proximity in the map mirrors closeness in real life, making relationships feel spatial and alive
- Gestures on the constellation trigger actions — check-ins, protection status, AI layer — the map is the interface

---

## The Companion
https://trendlife-constellation.vercel.app/aibutler-spec.html

- Butler initiates; user just responds — conversational flow replaces navigation
- Context cards surface at the right moment with one-tap actions, no screen-opening needed
- The layout adapts automatically to each member's settings — font size, interaction density, and content depth shift so every family member gets a UI built for them, not adjusted by them
