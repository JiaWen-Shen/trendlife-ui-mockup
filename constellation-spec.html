Member's View

A personal digital space where the individual member sees herself at the center of her family network. The AI Guardian Arc monitors silently above; family nodes reflect connection and care. Tapping navigates to health data, voice, and relationship depth.

✦
Grace Chen · 68 years old
Retired homemaker · Taipei · Grandma in the Chen family
Senior member Tech-light Health-conscious Fraud-vulnerable Family-connected
Design Principles
1
Self at center — Grace sees herself surrounded by her loved ones, not managed by them. The design reinforces her agency and presence within the family network.
2
Guardian Arc as ambient shield — The AI Butler arc at the top conveys protection without intruding. It's always present, quietly watching — tapping reveals the AI's actions for the day.
3
Relational depth on tap — Each family node is a doorway: voice messages, shared memories, or live health updates. Navigating is exploratory, not task-driven.
4
Health as context, not alarm — The health report is accessible but presented calmly. Data is shown with context and AI recommendations, never as raw numbers without meaning.
5
Voice as primary input — For seniors, voice is more natural than tapping. The Your Voice screen allows hands-free navigation and messaging.

Manager's View

A caregiver command center where Eva monitors all family members from the center of the network. Alerts surface proactively at the top right; each member node leads to their protection or health report; the AI Butler coordinates complex tasks through a dedicated chat.

E
Eva Chen · 45 years old
Brand Manager · Taipei · Family coordinator for Wei, Yuki, Ethan & Grandma
Working mom Primary caregiver Health manager Risk-aware Delegates to AI
Design Principles
1
Eva at the center — As the primary caregiver, Eva sees herself surrounded by her family. Each node represents a relationship she actively monitors, not just a contact list.
2
Proactive alerts — Three stacked alert pills appear at the top right, surfacing urgent issues before Eva looks for them. Each is independently dismissible once reviewed.
3
Per-member depth — Tapping any family node navigates to that person's dedicated protection or health report. No member's status is more than one tap away.
4
Guardian Arc as action gateway — The arc navigates directly to AI Butler chat — the highest-frequency action for Eva as a coordinator. The arc is both a reassurance symbol and a shortcut.
5
Events as cross-member audit trail — The middle tab shows a chronological family-wide activity log, giving Eva a single view of what happened across everyone without opening each profile.
