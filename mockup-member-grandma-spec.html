Manager's View

A family command-center experience where the household manager gets an AI-powered dashboard across all family members — surfacing health alerts, blocking risks, executing tasks, and coordinating everyone's day from a single screen.

E
Eva Chen · 45 years old
Brand Manager · Taipei · Family coordinator for Wei, Yvonne, Ethan & Grandma
Working mom · 4-person household Manages Grandma's health Oversees children's schedule Delegates to AI Risk-aware decision maker
Design Principles
1
Family as dashboard — The home screen shows all four members' live status via IG-style story bubbles. Each bubble is an AI-authored micro-summary of what's happening right now.
2
Two-tab command center — Home tab holds the task list and family status grid; AI Butler tab gives a direct conversation channel without leaving the app.
3
AI-planned execution — Every to-do item arrives with AI-drafted execution steps. Eva reviews and confirms; AI handles the follow-through — calls, payments, bookings, reminders.
4
Risk-first surfacing — Health flags, blocked fraud attempts, and anomalous payments are elevated above routine tasks. Risks always appear before Eva needs to look for them.
5
One-tap depth per member — Tapping a story bubble opens a message history; tapping the avatar opens that member's AI work summary — two distinct entry points per person.

Teens View

A social-native experience where AI Butler joins LINE group chats to assist with content creation, intercept security threats, and auto-exit — built for young creators who move fast and value privacy.

Y
Yvonne Chen · 16 years old
High School Student · Taipei · IG Lifestyle Creator
IG Creator · 12K Followers LINE Power User Trend-chaser Group Admin Privacy-conscious
Design Principles
1
Social-native AI — The butler joins Yvonne's existing LINE group as a temporary collaborator. No new app, no onboarding — AI meets users where they already are.
2
Privacy by default — AI announces its full data policy the moment it joins. It only responds when @-mentioned, never stores group chat history, and exits automatically.
3
Auto-exit mechanism — AI proactively asks if there are remaining questions before leaving, then exits after 5 minutes of inactivity. Users can always re-invite it at any time.
4
Actionable creative outputs — Every task generates a shareable, production-ready deliverable: IG design files, curated image links — all saved to Yvonne's TrendLife library for 7 days.
5
Active security shield — AI proactively scans every link shared in the group and blocks phishing attempts in real time, alerting all members before anyone can click.
